Important limitations:
- This is a browser-local frontend Beta, not a production HR system.
- Data is stored in the current browser and is not synchronized across devices.
- Temporary credentials are visible in the frontend bundle and are not secure authentication.
- Logto, Supabase, Google, LINE, NFC, QR, device binding and production APIs are not connected.
- Premium and Performance planned features are labeled as not yet connected in Beta.
